### [Applying the noiseless output extrapolation error mitigation protocol to calculate real time quantum field theory scattering phase shifts](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevD.109.014505)
*Physical Review D*

**Zachary Parks**, Arnaud Carignan-Dugas, Erik Gustafson, Yannick Meurice, Patrick Dreher

Using the noiseless output extrapolation (NOX) protocol, we improved the accuracy of real-time scattering calculations on noisy intermediate scale quantum (NISQ) hardware. The results confirm NOX improves the accuracy of physics simulations such as these, even on cloud-accessible quantum hardware.

### [Measuring qubit stability in a gate-based NISQ hardware processor](https://doi.org/10.1007/s11128-023-03826-4)
*Quantum Information Processing*

Kubra Yeter-Aydeniz, **Zachary Parks**, Aadithya Nair, Erik Gustafson, Alexander Kemper, Raphael Pooser

We conducted an in-depth study of coherent errors on a quantum hardware platform using a transverse-field Ising model Hamiltonian as a sample user application. Through a detailed set of measurements, we identify inter-day and intra-day qubit calibration drift and the impacts of quantum circuit placement on groups of qubits in different physical locations on the processor.
